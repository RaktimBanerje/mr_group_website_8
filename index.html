About Us

We, Mother Mary Institute of Nursing have established a milestone in the nursing education industry with our quality approach, constant research, and innovative development. We have created a model study approach in this nursing education as one of the best nursing institutes in India. We always teach our students to be responsible caregivers and become a devotee as nursing professionals with ethical standard maintenance.

To provide the best aspect to the students, we always provide standard nursing education both theoretically and practically.

Practical approaches at our laboratory provide great mileage to students in their future employment.

We have 6 months of internship programming to provide hands-on job experience and make students professionally experienced.
